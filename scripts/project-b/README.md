This is the "second DPS IS A LIE" batch, five times over, per the owner's brief: incredible, exurb1a-heavy, end to end.

## Why these five

- **Selected by seed breadth first.** The batch was chosen against the 50K formula (searched noun + universal belief overturned + three-word thumbnail legible cold + standalone + checkable) after two candidates were cut by the owner and one (travel friction) was cut in drafting for colliding with Project W's boat essay. Replacements (no-dailies, clues) were picked to stay in the *design argument* lane — the channel's proven identity — and away from the nostalgia/vibes lane (the catalogue's weakest performer).
- **Zero overlap, checked against everything.** Ironman flanks the central-bank draft (money) and bank-autobiography (possessions) without touching either's argument — it argues about *meaning under markets*. No-dailies is scheduling/FOMO where attention-tax is intensity pricing; Tears of Guthix keeps the weekly-ritual material (one line max here). Clues wire in the Scroll Box update as the turn — news the essay metabolizes rather than reports. Classless touches the trinity where Project W's raiding essay touched institutions — different game, different layer. Pets circles the luck/measurement territory of Dry Anonymous and the collection log at a deliberate distance: dry is the wait, the log is the ledger, this is the object.
- **The callback web stays light by design** — wide-seed videos must never read as homework. Each script carries at most two one-line callbacks, all cuttable per the standing rule.

## Format spread

Monastery / voicemail inbox / self-solving clue trail / census / appraisal table. Nothing reused from V (museum, tour, split-screen, trial, funeral), W (anatomy, voyage, onboarding, briefing, lab report), V2 (dig, appointment, support group, noir, nature film), N (cave tour, field trip, restaurant review, surveyor's report, weather forecast), or F (manual, bedtime story, reference letter, crash investigation, meditation, plumber, magic trick, biography). Two `[hold]`s in the batch (#2's empty message, #5's verdict). One profanity each in #2–#5, placed at register peaks; #1 runs clean (monastic register unbroken, same exception as Tears of Guthix).

## Suggested release order

1. **#4 (State Your Class)** — the widest cold seed in the batch; legible to every RPG player ever; the strongest candidate for a second breakout.
2. **#1 (The Order of the Grey Helmet)** — biggest in-community audience (ironman opinions are mandatory); the GE-heresy argument is prime comment bait.
3. **#5 (It Does Nothing)** — the paradox is instantly felt by anyone with a pet or a dry streak; best shorts material (the two-questions beat).
4. **#3 (The Interruption)** — **time to the stackable-clues conversation** if it's still warm; the update hook decays, the essay doesn't.
5. **#2 (Nothing Is Waiting for You)** — the crossover play; benefits from the channel having a few wide videos out so the algorithm can find the non-OSRS audience it addresses.
