## Why these five

- **Two seeds claimed from the backlog.** "In Defense of Going Dry" and "Slayer solves the problem of what do I even do today" have been sitting in `ideas/backlog.md` as sparks since 2026-07-03; Project V explicitly left them untouched, and #3 and #2 here are their full pipelines (backlog statuses updated). The other three (bank, bots, Leagues) are new territory.
- **Zero overlap with the spent wells.** The bank essay is about *curation and memory*, with a wired-in callback to the 28-slots video (which argued the mechanical genius of the constraint; this one reads the container as a psychological document). Going Dry argues about *pity and luck* where Project V's collection log argued about *measurement* — the adjacency Project V's README already licensed. Bots and Leagues touch nothing in the catalogue, the drafts folder, or Project V.
- **The V1 callback web extends.** #2 reuses the "never re-tuned / can't optimize against you" argument shape from *92 Is Half of 99* and says so on camera; #3 can lean on the ledger's WHO gaming-disorder entry; #5 runs on two already-ledger-verified population stats — the only batch script anywhere with verified numbers in its header from day one.
- **Every thesis passes the strategy note.** Widely-held belief overturned, standalone: "your bank is clutter" (it's a diary), "Slayer is a chore list" (it's the game's recommendation engine, and that's praise), "going dry is bad design" (it's the load-bearing refusal), "bots ruin the world's aliveness" (the aliveness survives them, which is the mystery), "Leagues is a side mode" (it held the game's all-time population record for eight months).

## Format spread

Dig site / doctor's office / support group / noir precinct / nature film. No container reuse from V (museum, tour, split-screen, trial, funeral) or W (anatomy, voyage, onboarding, briefing, lab report). The support group and the nature film push the tender register hardest; the noir and the doctor's office carry the most comedy; the dig sits in the middle. One `[hold]` in the batch — #5's mandala beat — same rationing as V and W.
